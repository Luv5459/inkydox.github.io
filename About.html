Company Introduction

InkyDox was founded in 2021 by Luv Kapoor. It is an international media and communication agency focusing on providing digital media/marketing services. 
Deeply engaged in the digital communication industry from the start, its services include- social media marketing/managing, Handling public relations, brand and business development, sales support, worldwide online/offline media, copywriting, graphic designing. Covering over 5+ countries and regions INDIA , UAE , USA , UK and others. It has established in depth cooperation with young and innovative individuals from India, committed for providing one stop solution for digital drawbacks.
